Review cards

What does HTML stand for?
HyperText Markup Language
How do you make a paragraph?
<p>...</p>
How do you make a heading?
<h1>...</h1>
How do you make a link?
<a href="url">...</a>
How do you add an image?
<img src="image.jpg" alt="...">
What does CSS do?
Styles web pages
How do you add audio?
<audio controls src="sound.mp3"></audio>
How do you add video?
<video controls src="movie.mp4"></video>
